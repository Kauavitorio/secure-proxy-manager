SSL Inspection & Certificate Management

SSL inspection (HTTPS filtering) allows the proxy to decrypt HTTPS traffic for content filtering. This requires installing a trusted certificate on all client devices.

Important: SSL inspection is an advanced feature that breaks the end-to-end encryption of HTTPS connections. Only use in environments where you have explicit permission to inspect encrypted traffic.
